Self-serve onboarding

Self-serve onboarding process empowers new users to get started quickly and easily, with minimal assistance needed. This streamlined approach ensures a seamless and efficient transition, enhancing user satisfaction from day one.

Professional Wall

This interactive platform encourages employee participation and strengthens connections within the organization. It serves as a visual representation of company culture, fostering communication, collaboration, and morale among team members.

Manage documents

Roles-based document management in HRMS ensures that employees have access to relevant documents based on their specific roles and permissions.

Analytics & Reporting

Reports and dashboards provide real-time insights and analytics, helping managers make informed decisions quickly. They offer a comprehensive view of key metrics, trends, and performance indicators, enhancing strategic planning and operational efficiency.

Incremental Design & Feedback
We followed an iterative approach wherein system was developed in small, manageable stages or increments. Each increment typically adds new features or functionality, building upon the previous work. This method allows for continuous feedback, adaptation to changing requirements, and early delivery of usable components, reducing risks and improving overall quality and efficiency.
